Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026-04-23

We do not collect personal data and we do not use advertising.

Anonymous technical statistics help us improve app quality and reliability.

What we collect

  • Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics), such as stack traces and basic device/app version info.
  • Anonymous usage events needed to improve onboarding:
    • whether notifications are enabled
    • whether the app is opened

What we do not collect

  • No names, emails, phone numbers, or other direct identifiers.
  • No advertising identifiers for ad targeting.
  • No data sale to third parties.

Why we collect this

  • To detect crashes faster and fix bugs.
  • To understand whether users enabled notifications and opened the app.

Data minimization

  • Data is processed in aggregated/anonymized form where possible.
  • We only use analytics required to maintain and improve the app.
